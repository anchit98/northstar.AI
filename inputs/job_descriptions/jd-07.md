## About the Job

# Role Overview

As a Technical Product Manager, you will execute the roadmap for key technology products while collaborating closely with cross-functional teams across the entire product lifecycle.

The role requires strong technical depth, product thinking, stakeholder management, and hands-on collaboration with Engineering, Architecture, UX, and Business teams.

You will:
- Define and prioritize requirements
- Drive product execution
- Lead backlog management and roadmap planning
- Support scalable integrations and API-driven platforms
- Deliver high-quality technology products aligned with customer and business needs

---

# Responsibilities

## Product Understanding & Customer Focus
- Develop deep understanding of the business, customers, platform users, and product challenges.
- Analyze customer behaviors, needs, and pain points to improve product outcomes.

## Product Roadmap & Backlog Management
- Collaborate with Product Managers on roadmap planning and integration initiatives.
- Prioritize features and maintain a healthy, well-structured backlog.

## Product Specifications & User Stories
- Create detailed product specifications explaining both functionality and business rationale.
- Write comprehensive user stories with detailed acceptance criteria.
- Incorporate security, compliance, and non-functional requirements into product definitions.

## Cross-Functional Collaboration
- Partner closely with Engineering, UX/UI, Architecture, and other stakeholders.
- Act as a subject matter expert within the product domain.

## Product Delivery & Agile Execution
- Lead the end-to-end product lifecycle from concept to launch.
- Participate in PI Planning, Sprint Planning, Standups, Backlog Grooming, and Product Demos.

## Technical Collaboration
- Work closely with engineering teams on integrations, system design, and platform scalability.
- Discuss trade-offs involving performance, scalability, reliability, and cost.

## Analytics & Product Performance
- Use analytics and customer insights to guide decision-making and product iteration.
- Monitor KPIs and product adoption metrics.

## Stakeholder Communication
- Present roadmap updates, progress reports, risks, and mitigation plans to leadership and stakeholders.

## Innovation & Product Thinking
- Contribute to a culture of experimentation, innovation, and continuous improvement.
