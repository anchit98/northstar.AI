# Product Manager I – Meesho

## About the Team

Product Management at Meesho is deeply rooted in customer obsession and rapid growth.

Meesho has built a strong e-commerce presence across Tier 2, Tier 3, and Tier 4 cities in Bharat by focusing on customers who are often shopping online for the first time.

The product organization emphasizes:
- User-first thinking
- Problem discovery over feature shipping
- Fast execution with measurable impact
- Deep understanding of customer behavior

Nearly 5% of Indian households shop with Meesho, making scale, accessibility, and customer empathy central to product development.

As a Product Manager I, you will work closely with:
- Engineering
- Design
- Data Science
- Analytics
- Business
- Operations

to solve large-scale customer and seller problems.

---

# About the Role

Meesho is looking for a Product Manager I who thrives in fast-paced startup environments and enjoys solving high-impact customer problems with a high degree of ownership and autonomy.

This is a cross-functional leadership role involving collaboration across:
- Software Engineering
- UX Design
- Marketing
- Category Management
- Operations
- Finance

You will drive product discovery, execution, experimentation, and business impact across key e-commerce experiences.

---

# What You Will Do

## Market & Customer Understanding
- Deeply understand customer and seller behavior through:
  - User research
  - Customer interviews
  - Market analysis
  - Competitive analysis
  - Quantitative and qualitative insights

## Problem Identification & Prioritization
- Break down complex business and customer problems into structured problem statements.
- Prioritize short-term and long-term opportunities based on impact.

## Solution Discovery
- Work with Design, Engineering, and Business teams to identify technology-driven solutions.
- Experiment with MVPs, prototypes, and iterative solution approaches.

## Product Delivery
- Define detailed product requirements and collaborate cross-functionally to launch products.
- Ensure product quality through:
  - User Acceptance Testing (UAT)
  - Dogfooding
  - Iterative validation

## Go-to-Market Planning
- Collaborate with Marketing and Business teams to maximize product adoption and success.

## Metrics & Product Optimization
- Define and track product metrics continuously.
- Analyze usage patterns and drive improvements through data-backed actions.

## Product Roadmapping
- Create and maintain a forward-looking product roadmap focused on key customer and business problems.
