# Product Manager – Growth Platforms | PhonePe

## About PhonePe

PhonePe is one of India’s largest digital payments and financial technology platforms.

Launched in August 2016, PhonePe has:
- 60+ Crore (600M+) registered users
- 4+ Crore merchants on its payments network
- 330M+ daily transactions
- Annualized TPV exceeding INR 150 lakh crore

PhonePe’s ecosystem includes:
- Digital payments
- Insurance
- Lending
- Wealth products
- Hyperlocal commerce via Pincode
- Indus AppStore for the Android ecosystem

The company’s mission is to unlock financial access and digital services for every Indian.
