# About the Company

Indico Labs helps Market Research agencies streamline reporting and storytelling workflows.

Its flagship product, **SlideGen**, is a self-service data visualization and report automation platform that enables users to generate PowerPoint reports directly from Excel data.

The platform focuses on:
- Automating report generation
- Simplifying data visualization
- Improving researcher productivity
- Reducing manual reporting effort by up to 80%

Indico Labs aims to transform how research insights and data stories are created and presented.

# Role Overview

As a Product Manager, you will oversee the complete product lifecycle from planning and discovery to development and delivery.

You will work closely with cross-functional teams to:
- Gather and prioritize product requirements
- Align product strategy with market needs
- Drive product enhancements and innovation
- Ensure successful delivery of customer-centric solutions

The role requires strong stakeholder management, analytical thinking, and the ability to operate independently in a remote-first environment.
