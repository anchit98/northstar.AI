# Senior Product Manager – Scapia

## About Scapia

Scapia is building a travel-focused credit card and consumer fintech ecosystem designed to turn everyday spending into travel experiences.

The company focuses on:
- Customer delight
- Fast execution
- Product innovation
- Travel-first financial experiences

Scapia operates with a highly collaborative and ownership-driven culture where teams brainstorm, debate, and iterate rapidly to build meaningful consumer products.

---

# About the Role

Scapia is hiring a Senior Product Manager to lead high-impact consumer product areas across multiple business verticals.

Depending on business priorities and candidate strengths, the role may focus on:
- Travel experiences
- Credit cards & payments
- UPI & commerce
- Rewards & loyalty
- Growth & engagement
- AI-driven customer experiences
- New 0→1 business initiatives

You will work closely with Engineering, Design, Data, Marketing, Operations, Risk, Compliance, and Leadership teams to define strategy, execute quickly, and drive measurable business outcomes.

- Use qualitative and quantitative insights for product decisions.
- Define, monitor, and optimize product KPIs.

## Cross-Functional Leadership
- Partner across Engineering, Business, Risk, Compliance, Operations, and Marketing teams.
- Align stakeholders and drive execution in fast-moving environments.

## AI & Innovation
- Explore AI-driven experiences across:
  - Personalization

  - Discovery
  - Customer engagement
- Help shape next-generation consumer experiences powered by emerging technologies.
