# Role Overview

The Product Manager – Data Products supports Ralph Lauren’s enterprise Data Product strategy through analytics, operational rigor, and stakeholder collaboration.

The role works closely with:
- Data Product Managers
- Data Engineering teams
- Analytics teams
- Governance teams
- Business stakeholders

to improve the adoption, measurement, quality, and continuous optimization of enterprise data products.

As part of Ralph Lauren’s digital and AI transformation initiatives, the role contributes to products powering:
- Predictive buying
- Demand forecasting
- Personalization
- Omnichannel customer experiences

This is a highly analytical and cross-functional role focused on translating business needs into measurable insights and scalable data product outcomes.

---

# Key Responsibilities

## Product Analytics & Performance Tracking
- Define and maintain KPIs, dashboards, and success metrics for data products.
- Track:
  - Product adoption
  - Usage trends
  - Availability
  - Data quality
- Analyze whether products are delivering expected business outcomes.
- Prepare insights for roadmap reviews and stakeholder discussions.

## Business & Stakeholder Support
- Collaborate with stakeholders to understand data and decision-making needs.
- Translate business questions into analytical requirements.
- Support communication through data-backed insights and impact assessments.

## Value Measurement & Insights
- Track business value realization including:
  - Efficiency gains
  - Decision support improvements
  - Process optimization
- Maintain baseline and post-launch performance measurements.
- Identify early indicators of:
  - Low adoption
  - Data quality issues
  - Product misalignment

## Product Lifecycle Support
- Support data products from discovery through launch and iteration.
- Ensure analytical readiness for launches and experimentation.
- Partner with Engineering and Analytics teams to validate metric definitions and outputs.
- Document learnings and improvement opportunities.

## Data Quality, Governance & Trust
- Work with Governance and Engineering teams to monitor:
  - Data quality
  - Consistency
  - Reliability
- Ensure metrics are interpretable and aligned with agreed definitions.
- Surface risks related to data gaps or inconsistencies.
- Support transparent and responsible data usage practices.
