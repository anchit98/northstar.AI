# Associate Product Manager (Finance Operations) – zenda

## Job Details

- **Role:** Associate Product Manager (Finance Operations)
- **Location:** Bangalore
- **Experience:** 2–4 years
- **Industry:** Fintech / E-commerce / Payments

---

# Role Overview

zenda is hiring a detail-oriented and proactive Associate Product Manager to join the Finance & Payments team.

This role sits at the intersection of business operations and technology, offering a high-growth pathway into core Product Management.

Initially, the role is operationally intensive and hands-on, involving ownership of payment gateway configurations, transaction reconciliations, and operational troubleshooting to ensure smooth fund flows. Over time, the focus shifts toward productizing and automating these workflows into scalable systems and internal products.

This role is ideal for candidates with strong business acumen, structured problem-solving ability, and an interest in building operationally scalable fintech systems.

---

# Responsibilities

## Reconciliation & Dispute Management
- Own daily reconciliation of payment gateway transactions with internal systems and bank statements.
- Identify discrepancies, investigate failed payments, and manage chargebacks.

## Autopay & Gateway Operations
- Manage recurring payment operations end-to-end.
- Handle Autopay configurations, debit scheduling, gateway setups, and KYC processes.
- Monitor operational health of recurring payment flows.

## Integration & System Monitoring
- Monitor critical system integrations proactively.
- Investigate sync failures, posting issues, and data inconsistencies across internal and third-party systems.

## Product Development & Stakeholder Collaboration
- Collaborate with Engineering, Design, and Business teams to convert operational challenges into product requirements.
- Support feature conceptualization and rollout initiatives.

## Cross-Functional Collaboration
- Coordinate with payment gateway partners, Engineering, and Finance teams to resolve settlement and operational issues.

## Reporting & Analytics
- Maintain and optimize MIS reporting across daily, weekly, and monthly cadences.
- Analyze payment failure and refund trends to identify process improvements.

## SLA & Compliance Management
- Monitor settlement cycles and ensure SLA adherence.
- Support compliance with internal controls and audit requirements.

## Process Automation
- Document manual workflows and identify operational bottlenecks.
- Partner with Engineering teams to automate repetitive operational processes.

## Stakeholder Management
- Work with multiple stakeholders to drive new features and operational enhancements.
