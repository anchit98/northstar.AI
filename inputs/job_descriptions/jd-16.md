# AI Product Manager – ALLEN Digital

## About ALLEN Digital

ALLEN Digital is building a technology-first education platform focused on transforming learning experiences through AI and personalization.

The company combines:
- Deep education expertise from ALLEN
- Advanced technology talent from leading tech firms
- Strategic backing from Bodhi Tree Systems

The mission is to solve key challenges in education such as:
- One-size-fits-all learning approaches
- Lack of personalized and holistic learning experiences

ALLEN Digital is leveraging AI to create an end-to-end ed-tech ecosystem that improves learning outcomes while making education more engaging, adaptive, and student-centric.

# About the Role

As an AI Product Manager, you will lead the development of AI-powered learning experiences designed to improve student engagement and educational outcomes.

The role focuses on building innovative AI applications across:
- Content generation
- Personalized study planning
- Interactive problem solving
- Test analytics
- Immersive learning experiences

You will work on creating intuitive and engaging product experiences that make AI a core differentiator for ALLEN Digital’s education platform.

The broader vision is to make education:
- Personalized
- Interactive
- Motivating
- Accessible across different proficiency levels
