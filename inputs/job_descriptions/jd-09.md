## Job Details

# About the Company

The company is an AI-native AdTech startup building a creative intelligence platform for marketers.

The platform leverages:
- LLMs
- RAG pipelines
- Autonomous AI systems

to analyze large-scale creative datasets and generate actionable marketing intelligence for global brands.

The product aims to automate complex manual creative analysis workflows into real-time AI-driven insights that influence high-value advertising decisions.

---

# Role Overview

As a Product Manager-II (Agentic AI), you will lead the design and execution of agentic AI systems focused on creative intelligence and marketing analytics.

You will work at the intersection of:
- AI product management
- LLM-powered systems
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Autonomous agentic workflows
- Data-driven decision systems

The role involves owning the end-to-end product lifecycle for AI-native products while collaborating closely with Engineering and Data Science teams to optimize production-grade AI systems.
