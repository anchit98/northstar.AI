# Product Manager – Groww

## About the Job

Groww is focused on making financial services accessible to every Indian through a multi-product financial platform. Every day, millions of customers use Groww to manage and grow their financial journey.

Customer obsession is deeply embedded in Groww’s culture. Every product decision, design choice, and algorithm is built with customer convenience and simplicity at its core.

At Groww, ownership, integrity, customer-centricity, and continuous innovation define the culture. Teams are empowered with autonomy and flexibility to create meaningful impact while building strong careers.

---

# Our Vision

Every individual deserves the knowledge, tools, and confidence to make informed financial decisions.

Groww aims to empower every Indian through a cutting-edge multi-product platform offering a wide range of financial services.

The long-term vision is to become the most trusted financial partner for millions of Indians.

# Role Overview

At Groww, Product Managers do more than write PRDs, build mocks, plan sprints, and analyze data — they build products that reshape how millions of Indians invest and manage money.

As a PM at Groww, you will:
- Define product strategy grounded in customer needs, tradeoffs, and market dynamics.
- Collaborate cross-functionally with Engineering, Design, Operations, Analytics, Finance, and Compliance teams.
- Deliver measurable business and customer outcomes.
- Build scalable and intuitive user experiences for tens of millions of users.
- Contribute to Groww’s mission of helping a billion Indians grow financially.

---

# Qualifications

- 3–6 years of Product Management experience is mandatory.
- Prior fintech experience is preferred but not mandatory.
- Ability to identify user pain points and convert them into actionable product roadmaps.
- Comfortable using tools like Replit, Lovable, or Bolt to prototype and showcase ideas.
- Strong technical understanding with the ability to discuss HLDs and LLDs with engineering teams.
- Strong decision-making capability based on first principles and data.
- Hands-on proficiency in SQL and Sheets/Excel.
- Strong stakeholder management and cross-functional collaboration skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Genuine passion for solving customer problems at scale.
