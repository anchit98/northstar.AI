# About Adobe Advertising

Adobe Advertising is an enterprise-grade advertising platform used by global brands and agencies to manage billions of dollars in advertising spend across the world’s largest publisher networks.

The platform helps advertisers tackle challenges related to:
- Complex media planning
- Rising media costs
- Evolving consumer behavior
- Measurement and attribution gaps
- Governance and operational efficiency

Adobe Advertising focuses on delivering:
- Incremental performance improvements
- Better governance controls
- Smarter utilization of first-party brand data
- AI-powered advertising intelligence

The team works closely with leading global advertisers and agencies to build industry-first self-service advertising capabilities powered by marketing science and artificial intelligence.
