# AI Associate Product Manager – Pine Labs

## About the Role

Pine Labs is building the next generation of AI-powered commerce infrastructure.

The company is hiring AI Associate Product Managers (AI APMs) to work at the intersection of:
- Product thinking
- AI experimentation
- Customer problem solving
- Rapid prototyping

This is not a traditional Product Management role.

AI APMs will function as AI-native product builders responsible for:
- Identifying high-impact AI opportunities
- Building rapid AI prototypes
- Working directly with enterprise customers
- Scaling AI-first capabilities across Pine Labs’ platform

The role involves close collaboration with:
- Product teams
- Engineering teams
- Operations teams
- Enterprise customers

to rapidly discover, validate, and deploy AI-powered workflows.

---

# What You Will Do

## 1. AI Problem Discovery

Identify opportunities where AI can improve merchant workflows and operational systems.

### Responsibilities
- Explore AI use cases across:
  - Merchant products
  - Payments infrastructure
  - Internal operational tools
- Work with Product, Operations, and Support teams to identify automation opportunities.
- Conduct rapid problem discovery using:
  - Customer feedback
  - Operational workflows
  - Product and business data

### Goal
Uncover high-leverage AI opportunities across the organization.

---

## 2. Rapid AI Prototyping

Act as the first builder for AI-native product ideas.

### Responsibilities
- Build prototypes using:
  - LLMs
  - AI agents
  - Copilots
  - Automation tooling
- Ship quick proof-of-concepts to validate AI solutions.
- Partner with Engineering teams to productionize successful prototypes.
- Run experiments and iterate rapidly based on results.

### Goal
Move from idea → prototype → validated solution within weeks.

---

## 3. Forward Deployed AI Solutions

Work directly with enterprise customers to deploy AI-powered workflows.

### Responsibilities
- Partner with merchants and financial institutions to understand operational workflows.
- Design AI-enabled workflow solutions tailored to customer needs.
- Deploy pilots and refine them based on customer feedback.
- Convert customer learnings into scalable platform capabilities.

### Goal
Ensure Pine Labs’ AI products solve real-world enterprise problems.
