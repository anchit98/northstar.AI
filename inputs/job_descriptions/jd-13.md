# Product Manager – AI Hiring Platform | CodeRound AI

## About CodeRound AI

CodeRound AI is building an AI-native talent platform that matches top engineering and AI talent with high-growth VC-funded startups across Silicon Valley and India.

The platform enables candidates to:
- Apply once
- Access multiple interview opportunities
- Connect with remote and onsite roles at leading startups

CodeRound AI is focused on helping top-tier startups in the US, UAE, and India hire exceptional engineering and machine learning talent through AI-driven evaluation systems.
