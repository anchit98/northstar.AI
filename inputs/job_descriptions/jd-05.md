# Product Manager – B2C Classifieds / Marketplace

## Job Details

- **Role:** Product Manager (B2C Classifieds / Marketplace)
- **Experience:** 4–6 Years
- **Location:** Bangalore

---

# About the Role

We are looking for a Product Manager with experience building and scaling high-traffic B2C discovery and classifieds platforms.

The ideal candidate should have worked on consumer-facing marketplace ecosystems where listings, search, comparison, and lead generation are central to the user experience.

This role focuses on optimizing discovery journeys, driving organic growth, improving lead conversion funnels, and scaling engagement across listing-driven products.

---

# What We’re Looking For

- Experience working on automobile, real estate, or similar classifieds platforms.
- Exposure to products focused on browsing, comparison, and enquiry generation.
- Familiarity with content-heavy discovery ecosystems such as comparison engines, review platforms, or aggregators.

---

# Key Responsibilities

## Growth & Funnel Optimization
- Drive user acquisition, engagement, and lead generation across marketplace funnels.
- Optimize end-to-end journeys from listing discovery to enquiry and lead conversion.

## Organic Discovery & SEO
- Define and execute SEO/GEO strategies to improve organic traffic and search rankings.
- Build scalable discovery mechanisms for high-intent users.

## CRM & Retention
- Design and manage drip campaigns, retargeting workflows, and CRM journeys.
- Improve lead nurturing and re-engagement flows.

## Lead Quality & Conversion
- Enhance lead qualification, scoring, and conversion efficiency.
- Improve business outcomes through funnel optimization and experimentation.

## Content-Led Product Development
- Build and scale content-driven features to improve discovery, engagement, and user trust.

## Cross-Functional Collaboration
- Partner with Engineering, Design, Marketing, Analytics, and Business teams to deliver product improvements.

## Data & Experimentation
- Use analytics, A/B testing, and experimentation frameworks to drive continuous product optimization.
