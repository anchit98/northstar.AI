# Product Manager – Razorpay

## About the Job

Razorpay is one of India’s leading full-stack financial technology companies, powering the way businesses move, manage, and grow money. Founded in 2014 by Harshil Mathur and Shashank Kumar with a vision to simplify payments for Indian businesses, Razorpay has evolved into a fintech powerhouse driving India’s digital payments revolution.

Razorpay powers millions of businesses with a smarter, scalable financial stack that goes beyond transactions to help businesses build and grow.

From AI-native agentic payments and AI-assisted fraud detection to real-time risk intelligence, automated reconciliation, smart payouts, and predictive financial insights, Razorpay is embedding intelligence across its ecosystem to make money movement faster, safer, and more efficient.

In collaboration with banks, networks, regulators, and ecosystem partners, Razorpay is building industry-first fintech solutions shaping the next generation of financial infrastructure.

Across India, Singapore, and Malaysia, Razorpay products span seamless checkouts, payroll automation, payouts, and financial operations — redefining how money moves across Asia.

# Roles and Responsibilities

## Build Customer Empathy
- Regularly interact with customers to deeply understand their needs.
- Stay close to customer sentiment through qualitative and quantitative insights.

## Devise Product Strategy
- Define long-term product strategy and quarterly roadmaps.
- Drive product vision and measurable business impact.

## Conceptualize New Solutions
- Use a problem-first, data-driven, and iterative approach to product conceptualization.
- Prioritize initiatives based on long-term platform thinking and value creation.

## Build Products
- Collaborate with Engineering and ecosystem stakeholders to build customer-centric products.
- Create detailed product specifications and manage launch scope effectively.
- Ensure timely delivery of features and product launches.

## Launch Products
- Define and execute go-to-market strategies with Product Marketing teams.
- Act as the product advocate internally and externally.

## Ecosystem Collaboration
- Partner with ecosystem players to co-build innovative fintech products.
- Operate independently in ambiguous and fast-moving environments.
