# Senior Product Manager – Post Order Customer Experience

## About the Role

We are looking for a strategic and customer-obsessed Senior Product Manager to lead the Post Order Customer Experience charter.

In this role, you will own the customer journey after an order is placed — spanning order tracking, delivery communication, issue resolution, returns, refunds, and post-delivery support.

You will work closely with Engineering, Design, Operations, Logistics, and Customer Support teams to identify friction points, define product strategy, and deliver scalable customer experience improvements that drive satisfaction and retention.

---

# Key Responsibilities

## Own the Post-Order Journey
- Lead the end-to-end post-order customer experience.
- Improve workflows related to order tracking, delivery updates, refunds, returns, and customer communication.

## Customer Understanding & Problem Discovery
- Identify customer pain points through research, stakeholder feedback, and data analysis.
- Act as the voice of the customer across product decision-making.

## Product Strategy & Roadmap
- Define product vision, roadmap, and execution strategy aligned with business goals.
- Balance customer delight with operational scalability.

## Product Execution
- Write clear product requirements and prioritize roadmap initiatives.
- Lead cross-functional execution across Engineering, Design, Operations, and Support teams.

## Operational Problem Solving
- Simplify operational complexity into intuitive customer-facing experiences.
- Collaborate with logistics and support teams to proactively reduce order-related friction.

## Metrics & Optimization
- Own and improve key metrics such as:
  - Delivery NPS
  - Order cancellation rates
  - Support ticket volumes
- Continuously optimize through experimentation, feedback loops, and data insights.

## Stakeholder Leadership
- Align teams around a clear product narrative and execution plan.
- Influence cross-functional stakeholders without direct authority.

## Innovation & Long-Term Thinking
- Balance short-term improvements with long-term product differentiation and scalability.

## Product & Execution Skills
- Proven track record of building and shipping scalable, high-impact products.
- Strong product sense with structured problem-solving ability.

## Analytical Skills
- Data-driven mindset with strong analytical and decision-making capabilities.

## Communication & Stakeholder Management
- Excellent communication and stakeholder alignment skills.

## Work Style
