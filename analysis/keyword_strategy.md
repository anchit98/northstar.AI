## 2. Placement Rules (DEC-10)

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Jargon cap | ≤ 2 PM-specific terms per experience bullet |
| Context | Keywords tied to metrics or projects — not naked lists |
| Title signal | Header line: **Product Manager \| AI & Automation \| 0-to-1 Products** (DEC-23) |
| Structure | `docs/resume_structure.md` — do not reorder sections for keyword stuffing |
| Skills backup | Skills section holds keywords not repeated in every bullet |
