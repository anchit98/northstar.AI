## 1. Core Positioning (All Channels)

**One-liner:** AI-native Product Manager who ships internal and client-facing products end-to-end—from discovery and MVP to adoption metrics—not just requirements documents.

**Proof pillars:**
1. **Scale:** 200+ discovery cycles, 15+ cross-functional team, $5.8M savings, ₹1.5 Cr+ revenue impact
2. **AI-native:** LLM intake pipeline, mutual fund RAG chatbot, Groww Review Analyzer agent (agentic VoC, MCP)
3. **Metrics:** 80% adoption, 100% retention, 0% churn, 85–90% CSAT on delivered tools

---

## 2. Compensation Alignment (DEC-6)

| Tier | Range | When to use | Anchoring language |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Stretch** | ₹35–40 LPA | AI-first startups, Series B/C, strong PM + builder profile | "Market rate for AI-native PMs who ship and own metrics" |
| **Primary** | ₹30–35 LPA | Default for PM / Product Ops roles at growth startups | "Aligned with scope: discovery, delivery, adoption ownership" |
| **Floor** | ₹25–28 LPA + equity | Early-stage, first PM hire, high learning equity | "Open to equity-heavy structure if scope is true PM ownership" |

**Defensibility (EC-4.3):** Jump from ~₹20 LPA is justified by revenue impact (₹1.5 Cr+), cost savings ($5.8M), team scope (15+), and AI builder differentiation—not title inflation.

**Negotiation playbook:** See `outputs/interview_positioning.md` §3.
