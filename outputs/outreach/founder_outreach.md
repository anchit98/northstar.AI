# Founder Outreach Templates

> **Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-05-16
> **Purpose:** High-impact, direct messages to startup founders (Seed to Series B).

## Strategy Notes
Founders don't care about BA vs. PM titles. They care about: Can you ship? Can you handle ambiguity? Can you drive revenue/save costs? 
**Tone:** Bold, value-first, builder-executor.

---

### 3 Variant Openings (DEC-8)
*Choose one based on your research:*
- **Variant 1 (The Customer Angle):** `[PERSONALIZATION_HOOK: "I've been a user of [Product] for a few months and the recent update to [Feature] is a game-changer for [User Pain Point]."]`
- **Variant 2 (The Vision Angle):** `[PERSONALIZATION_HOOK: "I read your recent manifesto on [Topic] and completely agree that the future of [Industry] belongs to AI-native workflows."]`
- **Variant 3 (The Milestone Angle):** `[PERSONALIZATION_HOOK: "Congrats on crossing [Milestone/Funding/Launch]. Scaling the product team is usually the hardest part of this phase."]`
