# Recruiter Outreach Templates

> **Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-05-16
> **Purpose:** Concise, metric-dense messages for internal recruiters and agency headhunters.

## Strategy Notes
Recruiters are pattern-matching against Job Descriptions. Make it incredibly easy for them to check their boxes: PM experience, AI/Tech fluency, metrics, and scale.

---

### 3 Variant Openings (DEC-8)
*Choose one based on your research:*
- **Variant 1 (Direct Role Match):** `[PERSONALIZATION_HOOK: "I saw your post hiring for the AI Product Manager role at [Company] and noticed you're looking for someone with strong LLM integration experience."]`
- **Variant 2 (Company Growth):** `[PERSONALIZATION_HOOK: "With [Company]'s recent expansion into [Market], I imagine you're actively scaling the product team."]`
- **Variant 3 (Mutual Connection/Alumni):** `[PERSONALIZATION_HOOK: "I see we're both connected with [Name] / both part of the NextLeap community!"]`
