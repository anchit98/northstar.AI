# LinkedIn Messages & Networking

> **Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-05-16
> **Purpose:** Short-form DMs for networking, referrals, and follow-ups.

---

## 1. LinkedIn Outreach Volume Limits (EC-5.4)
**CRITICAL:** Do not trigger LinkedIn's spam filters.
- **Daily Limit:** Max 20–30 connection requests per day.
- **Weekly Limit:** Max 50–100 connection requests per week.
- **Strategy:** Prioritize quality over volume. Always include a personalized note. Spread your total outreach across Email and LinkedIn.

---

**For a Recruiter:**

## 3. The "Coffee Chat / Referral" DM (After connecting)

**Goal:** Get a 15-minute chat to learn about the company and secure an internal referral.

> Hi [Name],
> 
> Thanks for connecting! `[PERSONALIZATION_HOOK: I've been following [Company]'s recent launch of X and was really impressed by the UX.]`
> 
> I'm currently an AI-native PM at WPP Media (Meta Campaign Activation—35% FTE savings, ~₹1.5 Cr+ department savings) and shipped a mutual fund RAG chatbot used by 200+ people. I'm exploring new PM opportunities and [Company Name] is at the top of my list. 
> 
> I know you're busy, but would you be open to a quick 10-minute virtual coffee chat next week? I'd love to hear about your experience working there and the product culture. 
> 
> No worries if you don't have the bandwidth right now!
> 
> Best,
> Anchit
