## 1. Company Classification & Positioning Guide (EC-5.2)

Before sending *any* outreach, classify the target company and select the appropriate narrative variant from `analysis/positioning_strategy.md`:

| Company Stage / Type | Narrative Emphasis | Key Metric to Highlight |
|---|---|---|
| **Early-Stage (Pre-A to A)** | Scrappiness, execution speed, MVP building | "Shipped mutual fund RAG chatbot (200+ users, ≤3s)" |
| **AI Startups** | AI fluency, agentic AI, RAG | "Built mutual fund RAG chatbot & Groww Review Analyzer agent" |
| **Product-Led Growth (PLG)** | User-centricity, adoption, retention, experimentation | "Owned metrics: 80% adoption, 90% retention, 8–10% churn, 88% CSAT" |
| **Series B/C (Scaling)** | Process maturity, cross-functional ops, scaling | "Revamped intake pipeline—70% faster handoff, 100% acceptance across 250+ initiatives" |
| **Founder-Led Hiring** | Ownership mentality, bias for action, direct ROI | "Delivered Meta automation unlocking ₹1.5 Cr+ revenue impact" |

## 2. Follow-Up Cadence (EC-5.3)

**Rule:** Never send more than 3 touches unless the recipient engages.
- **Touch 1 (Day 1):** The initial pitch (Templates below).
- **Touch 2 (Day 5-7):** The Value-Add. Do not say "just bubbling this up." Share a relevant article, a quick product observation, or a congratulatory note on a recent launch.
- **Touch 3 (Day 10-14):** The Gentle Close. "Assuming this isn't a priority right now, I'll stop reaching out. Would love to stay connected for the future."

## 3. Hiring Manager (HM) Cold Email Templates

**Subject Line Ideas:**
- Product PM role / [Your Name] / [Company Name]
- Quick question about [Company]'s [Specific Product Feature]
- AI-Native PM for [Company Name]

### 3 Variant Openings (DEC-8)
*Choose one based on your research:*
- **Variant 1 (Product Insight):** `[PERSONALIZATION_HOOK: "I was testing the new [Feature Name] in your app and noticed how seamlessly it handles [X]. It got me thinking about how you're tracking activation for that flow."]`
- **Variant 2 (Company News):** `[PERSONALIZATION_HOOK: "Huge congratulations on the Series B announcement and the expansion into [Market]."]`
- **Variant 3 (Content/Talk):** `[PERSONALIZATION_HOOK: "I loved your recent podcast interview on [Topic]—your point about [Specific Insight] really resonated with my approach to building internal tools."]`

### The Core Pitch (Choose based on Company Classification)

**Option A: For AI / Early-Stage Startups**
> I'm an AI-native Product Manager currently leading product operations at WPP Media. I specialize in moving from ambiguity to shipped product fast—I built a production mutual fund RAG chatbot (ChromaDB, Groq, ≤3s cited answers) trusted by 200+ users, and delivered Meta Campaign Activation (35% FTE savings, ~₹1.5 Cr+ department savings). I'd love to bring this builder-executor mindset to [Company Name]'s product team.

**Option B: For Series B/C / Product-Led Startups**
> I'm a Product Manager currently leading product ops at WPP Media, where I focus on driving adoption and operational scale. I recently revamped our automation intake pipeline (LLM BRDs/SOWs, auto discovery, Wrike status)—70% faster handoff, 100% acceptance—and track 80% adoption, 90% retention, and 88% CSAT across delivered products. I see [Company Name] is scaling its [Specific Team/Product], and I'd love to bring my execution rigor to the team.

### The Call to Action (CTA)
> I've attached my resume for context. Are you open to a brief 10-minute chat next week to discuss how my background aligns with your roadmap?
