#### This is the most secure man who ever lived. Fans would drive past the house and find Carl Dean on the tractor in a straw hat. They'd ask if he was Dolly Parton's husband. He'd look at his work clothes and say "do I look like I'd be Dolly Parton's husband." Then go back to mowing. He married her in 1966, the year she won her first BMI award. He rented a tuxedo, sat through the ceremony, and on the way out told her he'd never attend another one. He kept that for the next 58 years. Sit with what that takes. His wife became one of the most famous people on the planet. Strangers showed up at his home to gawk. And he felt zero pull to be seen, zero need to correct anyone, zero resentment that the spotlight skipped him. He answered "are you Dolly Parton's husband" with a joke because the question genuinely didn't land on anything tender. Most people would have caved somewhere in there. Started coming to the award shows. Done one interview. Let it slip to the neighbor that yeah, that's my wife. The pull to borrow a little of that light is almost gravitational, and he never once leaned into it. Dolly noticed immediately. She said she knew right then she'd keep him private as best she could and never ask him to do anything. Her end of the deal was easy because his end was airtight. A man who doesn't want the fame can't be corrupted by it. They were married 58 years, until he died in 2024. She wrote I Will Always Love You. She built an empire. The whole time, the person at the center of her life was a man on a tractor who'd tell you to your face he couldn't possibly be married to Dolly Parton.

#### Cursor crossed $4 billion in annualized revenue with 40 engineers and one PM. At a traditional company, a single one of their product surfaces would get a squad of 15 to 20. The whole product org got rebuilt around three roles that barely resemble the old ones. The PM who used to write specs now curates outcomes. Once agents verify their own work at the point of generation and humans review the result instead of the line-by-line diff, the 30-page PRD is wasted motion.
