# Personal Branding Recommendations

> **Date:** 2026-05-15
> **Target Audience:** Startup Founders, Product Leaders, Tech Recruiters

---

## 1. Core Positioning Statement
**"The Execution-Obsessed, AI-Native Product Builder."**

You are not a traditional, theoretical PM who only writes PRDs and waits for engineering. You are a builder who owns **product discovery**, **product strategy**, **roadmap prioritization (RICE)**, and **end-to-end delivery**—from **MVP** to **UAT** and **adoption**. You track **KPI ownership** (adoption, retention, churn, CSAT), run **data-driven prioritization**, and ship **AI-native products** using Cursor, **LLM integration**, and **prompt engineering**. You bridge **cross-functional leadership**, **product operations**, and technical execution.

## 2. LinkedIn Profile Optimization

### Visuals
- **Profile Photo:** Ensure your photo is high-quality, approachable, and professional but modern (no stiff corporate ties; think startup casual).
- **Background Banner:** Create a simple, clean banner (using Canva/Figma) with a tagline like: *"Building Scalable AI Products & Automation Platforms"* or *"Turning Ambiguity into Shipped Products."*

### Featured Section
Pin your best case studies and portfolio links to the top of your profile:
1. **AI-Powered Restaurant Recommendation Engine** (AI-native product, RICE, prompt engineering, experimentation)
2. **Meta Campaign Setup Automation** (Phased roadmap, ₹1.5 Cr+ impact, cross-functional delivery)
3. **Client Reporting MVP + RLS Chatbot** (1-day MVP, natural language to SQL, operational scalability)

## 3. Content Pillars (For LinkedIn Posting)

To build inbound recruiter interest, post 1-2 times a week focusing on these three pillars:

### Pillar 1: AI-Assisted Product Management (The "How-To")
*Show founders you know how to build faster than traditional PMs.*
- **Ideas:** How you used Cursor to build a client reporting MVP in 1 day. How you integrate LLMs into your BRD/SOW workflows. The future of the PM role in the age of AI.

### Pillar 2: Product Operations & Scaling (The "Impact")
*Show hiring managers you understand the messy reality of scaling.*
- **Ideas:** Why internal tools are harder to build than B2C apps (adoption challenges). How to automate intake pipelines. The importance of tracking adoption and retention for internal products.

### Pillar 3: The Transition Journey (The "Hustle")
*Show your growth mindset and NextLeap learnings.*
- **Ideas:** Lessons learned from the NextLeap PM Fellowship. How your background in Sales and QA makes you a more empathetic Product Manager. Case study teardowns (like the Swish delivery optimization).

## 4. Networking Strategy (Pre-Phase 5)
- **Engage with Target Companies:** Start following and commenting on posts from product leaders at Series A-C Indian startups. Don't just "Like"—leave thoughtful comments that show your analytical mindset.
- **Join Communities:** Be active in the NextLeap community, The Product Folks, and relevant Slack/Discord groups for Indian PMs.
- **Build in Public:** When you update your portfolio or build a new MVP, share the *process*, not just the final link. Talk about the RICE prioritization or the prompt engineering challenges you faced.
